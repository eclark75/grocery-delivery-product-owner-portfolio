
# User Personas

## Persona 1 – Busy Professional

**Name:** Sarah Johnson

**Age:** 34

**Occupation:** Marketing Manager

### Goals
- Order groceries quickly after work.
- Save favorite grocery items.
- Schedule deliveries around work hours.

### Pain Points
- Doesn't have time to shop in person.
- Long checkout processes are frustrating.
- Wants reliable delivery times.

---

## Persona 2 – Retired Senior

**Name:** Robert Williams

**Age:** 68

**Occupation:** Retired Teacher

### Goals
- Easily find grocery items.
- Use large, easy-to-read screens.
- Receive groceries at home.

### Pain Points
- Small text is difficult to read.
- Complicated navigation is confusing.
- Wants a simple checkout experience.

---

## Design Decisions

These personas guided the design by emphasizing:

- Simple navigation
- Fast product search
- Large, readable interface
- Secure checkout
- Easy reorder functionality
